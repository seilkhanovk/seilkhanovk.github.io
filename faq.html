FAQ

We know your data is a very sensitive issue. That is why we prepared a list of most commonly asked questions...

What personal data does Rita store?

We only store your login credentials, email and password (encrypted) and the date and time you create your account. These credentials are securely stored on our cloud partner, AWS.



What anonymised data does leave the Rita App?

List of companies shared with Purpose: Restrict Access Feature We have built a database with email addresses of the Data Protection Officers of many companies. Our system needs to search for the company email in our database to support you in sending Right to be Forgotten requests to companies.

Where is my data stored?

Rita securely stores your data in an Postgres server provided by AWS located in AWS “Euro data center hub”, Ireland. We only store your login credentials, email and password (encrypted) and the date and time you create your account.
